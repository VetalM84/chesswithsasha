Meet the Master

Oleksandr Matlak (Sasha)

Oleksandr Matlak (Sasha) is an accomplished chess master and trainer from Kyiv, Ukraine. He has been living in Wells, Somerset for three years, developing local talent and bringing competitive chess to the region.
