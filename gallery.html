info Cloudinary Setup Required

To automatically pull your photos, this page connects directly to your Cloudinary account. Please ensure:

  1. You set your Cloudinary cloud name in gallery.js.
  2. In your Cloudinary Dashboard under Settings > Security, disable/uncheck the "Restrict API access - Resource list" option.
  3. Tag uploaded images with any of these 9 tags: chess-clubs, chess-player, events, lectures, references, somerset-media, students, teams, or with-top-players.
