Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

At ChessWithSasha, we take your privacy and the protection of your personal information very seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights concerning your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information directly provided by you during session booking, club registration, or email correspondences. This includes:

  • Contact details (Name, Email address, Phone number).
  • Student details (Age, Current chess skill level).
  • Parent/Guardian contact details (for junior students under 18).
  • Information relevant to schedules, notes, and specific student learning goals.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Coordinate, schedule, and deliver high-quality chess lessons.
  • Manage junior and after-school chess club rosters and attendances.
  • Send lesson updates, schedule adjustments, or club notices.
  • Address inquiries, billing, and support requests.

3. Data Sharing and Security

Your details are kept strictly confidential. We do not sell or trade your personal data. Your information (such as your name and email address) is only shared with third-party calendar engines (e.g., Calendly, Google Calendar) to enable integrations, or as required by UK law.

4. Child Protection & Safeguarding

As coach Sasha works heavily with children under 18, safeguarding is our top priority. We comply with all standard UK DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) checks. We only collect the minimal personal data required for children to register in after-school clubs, and always with explicit parental or school consent.

Terms of Service

Last updated: June 15, 2026

By registering for private coaching, attending camps, or joining our chess clubs, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms and conditions.

1. Lesson Bookings and Scheduling

  • Lessons must be scheduled in advance via the online calendar booking tool or direct email/WhatsApp agreement.
  • Lesson duration slots are strictly adhered to: 45 minutes for younger juniors, and 1 to 2 hours for older students or adults.

2. Cancellation and Rescheduling Policy

We respect your time and ask that you respect ours. Please review our policy:

  • 3-Hour Notice: Cancellations or requests to reschedule must be made at least 3 hours prior to the scheduled lesson time.
  • Late Cancellations: Lessons cancelled with less than 3 hours' notice will be charged at the full standard rate.
  • Coach Cancellations: If Sasha has to cancel due to tournament travel, FIDE duties, or illness, the lesson will be rescheduled at no additional cost.

3. Payments

  • All payments are cashless and must be made via bank transfer to a UK bank card/account or via PayPal.
  • If a session is booked directly through the website, payment must be completed prior to the start of the lesson.
  • Chess club fees are paid on a term-by-term basis at the start of each school term. No refunds are provided for missed club sessions.

4. Rules and Conduct

Students are expected to maintain respect, intellectual integrity, and good sportsmanship. Coach Sasha reserves the right to terminate coaching relationships or remove students from clubs in instances of disruptive behavior or bullying.
